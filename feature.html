Plans We Offer

We offer comprehensive managed services that include remote monitoring and antivirus protection to keep your systems secure. Our larger plans also feature advanced email filtering and phishing training to help safeguard against cyber threats and ensure your team is well-prepared.

For just $9.99 per workstation, you’ll receive remote monitoring, antivirus protection, and access to our 24/7 email support line to submit tickets for any issues.
